Raising a Generation Conformed to the Image of Christ

We are discipling the next generation through the Word of God, equipping youth with spiritual foundation, biblical knowledge, and Christ-centered character for life transformation.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

A generation transformed by Christ's image, standing firm in biblical truth, and equipped to impact the world with spiritual conviction and moral excellence.

Mission

To disciple youth through intensive biblical training, spiritual warfare awareness, and prayer-centered community, building a foundation of faith that transforms lives and honors God.

Our Journey

2014
Image of Christ was founded with a burden to reach and disciple youth in Wolaita Sodo, beginning with small group meetings focused on biblical foundation.
2015
First regional training organized, attracting youth from multiple communities. Spiritual warfare curriculum was developed and implemented.
2016
Established weekly prayer gatherings and launched youth resource articles on self-acceptance, friendship, and faith development.
2017 - Present
Continuous expansion with specialized trainings, mentorship programs, and digital outreach. Now serving hundreds of young believers across the region.

About Image of Christ

Our Story

Image of Christ was birthed from a deep conviction that today's youth need spiritual foundation, biblical knowledge, and Christ-centered mentorship. Founded in 2014, the ministry recognized a critical gap in discipleship—many young believers were navigating spiritual warfare without proper preparation or understanding of God's Word.

What began as small group meetings in Wolaita Sodo has grown into a vibrant, mission-focused community reaching hundreds of young people. Our approach combines intensive biblical training, practical spiritual guidance, and genuine community support.

The Challenge Youth Face

Spiritual Confusion

A generation bombarded with contradictory messages about identity, purpose, and faith needs clear biblical truth.

Spiritual Warfare

Young believers often lack awareness and preparation for the spiritual battles they face daily.

Lack of Community

Isolation weakens faith. Youth need authentic, supportive community with older believers as mentors.

Our Response: A Spiritual Foundation

We believe that every young person can be transformed by God's Word and the Holy Spirit. Through discipleship, we help youth:

  • Understand their true identity in Christ
  • Develop a deep, personal relationship with God's Word
  • Build spiritual awareness and resistance to deception
  • Connect with a community of believers who support their growth
  • Step into their purpose as young agents of transformation

Our Programs

We offer specialized discipleship programs designed to equip youth with biblical knowledge, spiritual strength, and community support.

Week-Long Intensive Trainings

Deep biblical studies covering Old Testament, New Testament, theology, and Christian living. Participants engage with God's Word in a structured, immersive environment designed for genuine spiritual growth.

Format: 5-day sessions · Multiple times yearly

Spiritual Warfare Teachings

Comprehensive curriculum on the reality of spiritual warfare, biblical discernment, and practical strategies for spiritual defense. Youth learn to identify deception and stand firm in Christ.

Format: Monthly sessions · Interactive discussions

Prayer Gatherings

Regular prayer meetings focused on intercession, worship, and community prayer. These gatherings build spiritual intimacy with God and strengthen the prayer life of young believers.

Format: Weekly meetings · Morning and evening options

Youth Articles & Resources

Self-Acceptance: Learning to see yourself through God's eyes and embracing your identity in Christ.

Friendship: Building godly friendships based on truth, accountability, and mutual spiritual growth.

Mentorship Community

One-on-one and small group mentoring relationships connecting experienced believers with young people. Mentors provide guidance, accountability, and spiritual modeling.

Format: Flexible meetings · Personalized guidance

Leadership Development

Training program for young believers called to leadership roles. Focuses on biblical leadership principles, spiritual character, and practical ministry skills.

Format: Semester-based · Hands-on training
